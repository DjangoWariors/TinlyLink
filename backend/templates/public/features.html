Link Shortening

Turn any URL into a clean, trackable link

Long URLs look messy in social posts, emails, and presentations. TinlyLink converts them into short, professional links that are easy to share and easy to remember. Every link you create is tracked automatically , you will know how many people clicked, from where, and on what device.

Custom slugs

Choose a memorable back-half like tinlylink.com/launch instead of a random string. Makes your links recognizable and trustworthy.

Bulk creation

Need to shorten hundreds of URLs at once? Import a CSV or use the API to create links in bulk. Each link gets its own tracking.

Expiration dates

Set links to expire after a specific date — perfect for limited-time offers, event registrations, or temporary access links.

Password protection

Add a password to any link so only authorized people can access the destination. Useful for client portals, private documents, or gated content.

QR Code Generator

Create QR codes that people actually notice

A branded QR code gets scanned more often than a generic black-and-white one. TinlyLink lets you customize colors, add your logo, and choose from multiple styles — so your QR codes look professional on any surface, from product packaging to event posters.

Brand colors and logo

Set foreground and background colors to match your brand exactly. Upload your logo to appear in the center of the QR code.

Multiple styles

Choose from square, rounded, or dot patterns. Pick the style that fits your design language — from sharp and modern to soft and approachable.

Dynamic QR codes

Change the destination URL without reprinting the QR code. The image stays the same, but where it points updates instantly. Perfect for printed materials.

High-res downloads

Export as PNG, SVG, or PDF. Every format is high resolution and ready for both digital screens and professional print.

Scan tracking

Every scan is tracked just like a link click. See when, where, and how often your QR codes are being used in the real world.

Click Analytics

Understand who clicks your links and why

TinlyLink tracks every click across every link you create. Instead of guessing which marketing channel works best, you'll have actual data — broken down by location, device, time, and referral source.

Geographic data

See clicks broken down by country and city. Know where your audience is concentrated so you can target your efforts accordingly.

Device and browser breakdown

See what percentage of your audience uses mobile vs desktop, and which browsers they prefer. Helps ensure your landing pages work where it matters.

Referrer sources

Know exactly which website, social platform, or app sent each click. Understand which channels are worth investing in.

Click timeline

View clicks by hour, day, week, or month. Identify peak engagement windows and schedule your posts and campaigns at the right times.

CSV export

Download your analytics data as CSV for custom reporting, presentations, or importing into your own BI tools.
