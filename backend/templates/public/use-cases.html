How People Use TinlyLink

Whether you are running marketing campaigns, building software, managing a small business, or working with a team here's how TinlyLink fits into your workflow.

For Marketers

Know which campaigns actually drive results

Marketing involves sharing links across many channels social media, email newsletters, paid ads, influencer partnerships, and more. Without proper tracking, you're guessing which channel performs best. TinlyLink gives you the data to stop guessing.

Create a short link for each channel, tag it with UTM parameters using the built-in campaign builder, and watch the results come in. Compare click-through rates between your Instagram bio link and your email newsletter link. See if your Twitter audience is more engaged on mobile or desktop. Identify which countries your LinkedIn posts reach.

Common marketing workflows

Social media campaigns

Create separate short links for each platform. Compare Instagram vs Twitter vs LinkedIn performance in one dashboard.

Email newsletter tracking

Track click-through rates from weekly newsletters and drip campaigns. See which links get the most engagement per send.

UTM-tagged links

Use the built-in UTM builder to tag source, medium, and campaign. No more manually appending query strings to URLs.

Branded links for trust

Use a custom domain so your links match your brand. People are more likely to click a link from yourbrand.co than a generic shortener.

api-example.js 
// Create a short link via the API
const response = await fetch('https://tinlylink.com/api/v1/links/', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': `Bearer ${apiKey}`,
    'Content-Type': 'application/json',
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    original_url: 'https://example.com/my-page',
    custom_slug: 'my-link',
  }),
});

const { short_url, slug } = await response.json();
console.log(short_url);
// â†’ https://tinlylink.com/my-link

// Fetch click analytics
const stats = await fetch(
  `https://tinlylink.com/api/v1/analytics/clicks/?link=${slug}`,
  { headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${apiKey}` } }
);
const { total_clicks, by_country } = await stats.json();

For Developers

Build link shortening into your product

If your app or service generates shareable URLs user profiles, content pages, referral links, receipts you can use the TinlyLink API to shorten them automatically. No need to build and maintain your own shortening infrastructure.

Our REST API supports creating, reading, updating, and deleting links with simple JSON requests. Bulk endpoints let you shorten hundreds of URLs in a single call. Analytics endpoints give you programmatic access to click data for dashboards and reports.

What you can build

CMS integrations

Auto-shorten URLs when content is published. Works with any CMS that supports webhooks or API calls.

Chat bots and automation

Generate trackable short links inside Slack bots, Discord bots, or Zapier workflows.

Custom analytics dashboards

Pull click data via API and display it in your own internal dashboards alongside other metrics.

Transactional emails

Shorten links in order confirmations, password resets, or notification emails. Track open rates via link clicks.

For Small Businesses

Connect your offline and online presence

Restaurants, retail shops, salons, real estate agents, event organizers if your business exists in the physical world, you need a way to bridge the gap between print and digital. QR codes on menus, signage, and business cards are the simplest way to do that.

TinlyLink makes it easy to create branded QR codes that point to your website, booking page, menu, or social profiles. Dynamic QR codes mean you can update the destination anytime change your seasonal menu or update event details without reprinting a single thing.

Practical examples

Restaurant menus

Print a QR code on table tents that links to your online menu. Update menu items or prices without reprinting the QR code stays the same.

Business cards

Add a branded short link or QR code to your card that points to your portfolio, booking page, or LinkedIn profile.

Product packaging

Link to setup guides, warranty registration, or customer support directly from the product box. Track scan rates across product lines.

Event promotion

Put QR codes on flyers and posters that link to your event page or ticket purchase. See how many people scanned from each location.

For Teams

Collaborate on links across your organization

When multiple people in your organization share links marketing, sales, support, partnerships things get messy fast. Who created which link? Where are the analytics? Which domain should we use?

TinlyLink team workspaces bring everyone's links into one shared space. Each team member can create links and QR codes, view collective analytics, and manage campaigns while keeping personal links separate. Role-based access means you control who can do what.

Team features

Shared link library

All team links are visible to every member. No more asking "who has the link to that page?"

Role-based access

Owners manage billing and settings. Admins can invite members. Members can create links and view analytics.

Email invitations

Invite teammates by email. They get a link to join your workspace existing users connect instantly, new users sign up first.

Separate personal and team data

Switch between your personal workspace and team workspaces. Links and analytics are cleanly separated.
